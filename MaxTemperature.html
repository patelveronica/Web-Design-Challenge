Max Temperature

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approchaes the equator (0 Deg. Lattitude). More interestngly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
